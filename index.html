Inner Healing Workbook

Module 03 • Activating Prayer Tool

Module Objectives

1.

Understand the need for inner healing prayer and ongoing spiritual hygiene.

2.

Take the next step forward in your own healing journey.

3.

Consider equipping yourself to offer inner healing prayer to others.

Pre-Work Questions

Reflect on your reading and answer the following questions to prepare for the session.

Session Notes

"He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds." - Psalm 147:3

Practice Inner Healing

Part 1: Guided Exercise

Encounter Jesus and ask Him about the place where He chose to meet you and how He wants to bring healing. Jot down what you hear below.

Part 2: Triad Groups

Share what you are comfortable sharing. Listen on behalf of others, seal what God did, and bless one another. Jot down notes on how to bless your partners below.

Forgiveness Exercise

Unforgiveness limits your experience of freedom. Walk through these steps to release the debt owed to you.

Read Your Declaration Aloud:

"I forgive [Name] for [The Offense]."

"I choose this day to no longer live in reaction to that which you have said and/or done against me."

"I release you from the prison of my unforgiveness. Even as I have been forgiven, so also in Jesus' name I forgive you."

After reading this, remember to bless the offender and dismiss any oppression.

Inner Healing Guidelines

1

Identify & feel the emotion.

2

Discover the root event: 'God, when was the first time I experienced this?'

3

Discern the lie believed: 'Jesus, what did I come to believe?'

4

Reveal the truth: 'Lord, what truth do You have to communicate with me about the lie?'

5

Experience His freedom. If bondage remains, ask if there is another lie.

6

Discover what Jesus was doing and feeling when the wounding event took place.

7

Extend & receive forgiveness (to others, yourself, or God).

8

Uncover & renounce vows made to protect yourself from pain.

9

Break any pronouncements made over you by an authority.

10

Surrender your burden to Jesus and ask what He will give you in return.
